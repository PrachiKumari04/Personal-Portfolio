Abstract: The research paper delves into the world of Xenobots, exploring their creation process, design principles, potential applications, and the ethical considerations surrounding their development. Key Finding:

  1. Xenobots are a new type of living machine created from frog stem cells and designed using artificial intelligence (AI).
  2. They are microscopic and can be programmed for various functionalities like movement, object manipulation, and even self-assembly.
  3. Xenobots hold immense potential for various applications in fields like medicine (drug delivery, microsurgery), environmental science (pollution cleanup, bioremediation), and agriculture (targeted pesticide use, plant monitoring).
